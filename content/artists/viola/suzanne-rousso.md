Suzanne Rousso has played viola for symphonies in Texas, Tennessee, New Mexico, and beyond.

She performed regularly with the NC Symphony, and was appointed Director of Education in May of 1999, overseeing all aspects of NCS's educational programs. Following a stint as Director of Operations and Education for the Portland (Maine) Symphony, she returned to NC.
